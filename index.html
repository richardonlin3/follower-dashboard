Start

Welcome to the Social Media Dashboard project! This open-source dashboard has been developed to assist offices and businesses in effectively monitoring, managing, and enhancing their social media presence. With this dashboard, you can keep an eye on all essential metrics, including follower counts, interactions, and more, to optimize your online marketing.

Project Overview

In today's digital world, a strong presence on social media is crucial to stay in touch with your target audience and increase brand awareness. This dashboard provides a simple and clear way to track the performance of your social media accounts in real-time. Our main goals for this project are:

- Versatility: We aim to create a dashboard that allows integration with various social media platforms, including Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and even TikTok.
- User-Friendliness: The dashboard is designed to be user-friendly, enabling users to effortlessly monitor key metrics and generate reports.
- Customizability: We want to ensure that the dashboard can be customized to meet the specific needs of your business or office.

Features
Our Social Media Dashboard will offer a range of features, including: Follower Count Display: Track the evolution of your followers on various social media platforms, including TikTok. Interaction Statistics: Easily view interactions, likes, comments, and shares at a glance. Real-Time Updates: The dashboard will regularly update data to ensure you always have the latest information. Customizable Reports: Create reports to analyze performance over time.
